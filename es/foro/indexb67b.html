Welcome to the new ShertonEnglish.com!!!!!

por Adolfo » Mié Jul 02, 2008 10:06 pm

Hello everyone, welcome to the new ShertonEnglish. After a year and a half of hard work, we have finally launched our renovated site.

In the early days of 2007 we began to plan what we hope to become one of the referral sites for the millions of students who are learning this wonderful language. It is initially oriented to Spanish speakers, but we will gradually incorporate other native languages, including Portuguese, French and Italian.

We wish to thank for the reviews, suggestions and contributions from the thousands of users who contacted us and left their email addresses so that we could keep them informed. Our apologies for the delay, but we believe that it will be worth having waited this long. Even so, we have reserved much more material, which will be added in the future.

This forum will allow you to become part of a community where you can make friends, ask questions, solve problems and share experiences with students and teachers of English from all over the world. We would like to count with your participation and contribution to this endeavor. We will be waiting for you there, expecting your first opinions.

Enjoy it!
Adolfo
Sherton Teacher
