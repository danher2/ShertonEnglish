Gracias por su pregunta con respecto a las clases inglesas. Muchos de mis estudiantes, como usted, quieren adelantar su carrera pasando el TOEFL pruebe, quizá como usted, ellos deben trabajar y no deben poder asistir a una sesión de clases----y su tiempo está limitado y precioso.

Usted puede tener clases mientras en el consuelo de su casa o oficina. Usted también está en mando completo de sus estudios, usted puede adelantar rápidamente cuando usted desea y gasta más tiempo con un asunto que usted no ha dominado.
Mi plan es simple: usted consigue una una sesión de cara-a-cara de hora conmigo donde nosotros practicamos conversación y repasamos el trabajo que usted ha hecho. Si, no es posible venir a la clase, esto puede ser hecho por teléfono. Usted estudiará un preprograme juego de lecciones, y donde yo lo juzgo necesario, yo le enviaré la información adicional y lecciones por email. Sherton inglés es una gran herramienta para aprender inglés básico; es un libro del texto interactivo muy bueno, pero ése es todos que es. Úselo---pero un maestro se exige tener éxito aprendiendo el idioma inglés para ser una herramienta útil. Otro punto importante, el maestro necesita tener inglés como su idioma nativo. Muchos estudiantes vienen a mí pasar el TOEFL que ha aprendido de español que habla a maestros. Desgraciadamente, les faltan muchos del esencial, es como si ellos necesitan empezar del principio. Inglés es mi idioma nativo; Yo soy un escritor publicado, yo tengo una educación universitaria y diez años de experiencia instrucción.
Por favor considere esto en sus estudios. Thank you for your inquiry regarding English classes. Many of my students, like you, want to advance their career by passing the TOEFL test, maybe as you, they must work and are not able to attend a session of classes----and their time is limited and precious.
I have designed a format of giving English language classes that saves time and rapidly advances your learning. I teach primarily, but not always, by the internet. You are able to have classes while in the comfort of your home or office. You are also in complete control of your studies, you can advance quickly when you desire and spend more time with a subject you have not mastered.
My plan is simple: you get a one hour face-to-face session with me where we practice conversation and review the work you have done. If, it is not possible to come to class, this can be done by telephone. You will study a preprogram set of lessons, and where I deem it necessary, I will send you additional information and lessons by email. Email will be the main manor of communicating for it is convenient and gives you practice in writing.
I charge 150 pesos per week for all these services and if you desire, a payment plan can be established. Remember these are private lessons so the entire resources are concentrated on you as an individual, not one of thirty in a class.

Sherton English is a great tool to learn basic english; it is a very good interactive text book, but that is all it is. Use it---but a teacher is required to be successful in learning the English language to be a useful tool. There is a need for conversation, assisted reading and writing, plus the human touch, which only a teacher can give you. Another important point, the teacher needs to have English as their native language. Many students come to me to pass the TOEFL who have learned from Spanish speaking teachers. Unfortunately, they lack many of the essentials; it is as if they need to start from the beginning. English is my native language; I am a published writer, I have a university education and ten years of teaching experience.
Please consider this in your studies. What I charge is a bargain, for example, if you are part of a class of thirty you get very little if none one-on-one attention for 110 pesos and hour. My one hour of one-on-one is ten times more valuable and specialized, just for you.
